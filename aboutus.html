About Us

At The Wize Company, we specialize in steel detailing and structural design, delivering precise, innovative, and reliable solutions for the construction industry. With years of experience, we transform complex structural concepts into accurate, buildable, and cost-effective designs, ensuring compliance with industry standards and safety regulations. Our expert team utilizes advanced CAD software, BIM technology, and 3D modeling to create detailed structural drawings, fabrication plans, and connection designs for commercial, industrial, and residential projects. We collaborate closely with architects, engineers, and contractors to optimize structural integrity, reduce material waste, and enhance project efficiency. At The Wize Company, we are committed to precision, quality, and timely delivery. Whether it’s a high-rise building, bridge, or industrial structure, our steel detailing solutions are designed to streamline fabrication, improve coordination, and minimize construction errors. Partner with us for cutting-edge steel detailing services that ensure your projects are delivered on time, within budget, and with exceptional accuracy.

About Us

Trusted Steel Detailing & Structural Drafting Experts

At TheWizeCompany, we are a team of expert steel detailers and structural drafters who are passionate about helping construction projects succeed. We have over 10 years of experience in the industry and have worked on a wide range of projects, from small residential buildings to large commercial structures. Our team is dedicated to providing high-quality steel detailing and structural drafting services that meet the needs of our clients. We use the latest technology and software to ensure accuracy and efficiency in our work, and we are committed to delivering projects on time and within budget. Whether you are a contractor, architect, engineer, or developer, we can help you bring your vision to life. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you with your next project.
